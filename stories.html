Selena's Story


I am a survivor, and this is my story. I was 16 years old when my best friend's father sexually assaulted me. It would be almost 20 years before I would tell anyone what happened. I am subsequently living with the effects of CPTSD and trauma associated with the event, which has led to isolation. It has hindered me from reaching my full potential, because fear is such a powerful motivator.

Keeping up appearances became extremely important, but impossible while letting others in. I lost friendships and many opportunities. But I am ready to stand, to learn the skills I lack, and to reach out and find a community of survivors like me.
